African Hair Braiding

Authentic African Hair Braiding Service

We are a team of skilled hair stylists who specialize in authentic African hair braiding. Braiding hair is an ancient art form in Africa, handed down through families over the course of generations. Our stylists are skilled in authentic African hair braiding and have created unique and beautiful hair designs for all our customers. We love what we do, and we have been serving the area with commitment and pride for over a decade.

Our Services Include:

  • African Hair Braiding
  • Senegalese Twist
  • Box Braids
  • Feed in Cornrows
  • Sew in Hair Extensions
  • Dreadlock Cornrows
  • Haircuts
  • Kinky Twist
  • Micro Braids
  • Goddess Braid
  • Ghana Braids
  • Ghana Cornrows
  • Retwist Dreads
